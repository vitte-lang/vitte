Editorial Style Guide (Vitte Book)

Book Summary Checklist Glossary

Voice

  • Be precise, concrete, and execution-oriented.
  • Avoid vague phrasing and automatic repetition.
  • Explain the intent before the syntax detail.

Target Length

  • Goal: 1 paragraph (3 to 5 lines).
  • Example: 1 main block + 1 invalid variant.
  • Why: 1 short paragraph (3 sentences maximum).
  • Quick check: 1 question + 1 expected answer.
  • Try this: 2 to 4 short exercises.
  • If you're stuck: 3 verifiable points.
  • Full chapter size: equivalent of 10 to 30 paper pages.
  • Calculation reference: 350 words/page.
  • Operational bounds: 3500 to 10500 words per chapter.
  • Automatic check: make book-length-check.

Example Format

  • Use a stable progression: entry -> treatment -> exit.
  • Always specify preconditions and invariants.
  • Add one explicit invalid case in every technical section.

Punctuation

  • Every bullet point ends with punctuation.
  • Homogeneous headings: ## ... in French.
  • Avoid exclamation points outside quotations.

Required Chapter Outline

  • ## Objectif
  • ## Exemple
  • ## Pourquoi
  • ## Test mental
  • ## À faire
  • ## Corrigé minimal

Required Keyword Outline

  • ## Définition
  • ## Syntaxe
  • ## Quand l’utiliser / Quand l’éviter
  • ## Exemple nominal
  • ## Exemple invalide
  • ## Erreurs compilateur fréquentes
  • ## Mot-clé voisin
  • ## Pièges
  • ## Utilisé dans les chapitres
  • ## Voir aussi

Anti-redundancy rule: do not duplicate ## Différences proches and ## Mot-clé voisin.

Keep only ## Mot-clé voisin.

Global Markup

  • Include a Prérequis banner at the top of the chapter.
  • Include a Voir aussi line linking related chapters.
  • Add a Checkpoint synthèse every 2 to 3 chapters.

Links and Paths

  • Reference the book with the docs/book/ prefix.
  • Prefer stable paths relative to the root repository.
