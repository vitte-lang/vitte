# Vitte Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

Vitte is committed to providing a welcoming, inspiring, and harassment-free experience for everyone. We are dedicated to fostering an open and inclusive community where people feel safe to participate, whatever their identity or background.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

- **Being respectful** - Value each other's opinions, experience, and contributions
- **Being inclusive** - Welcome people of all backgrounds and perspectives
- **Being constructive** - Provide and accept feedback gracefully
- **Being collaborative** - Work together towards common goals
- **Being professional** - Maintain professionalism in all interactions
- **Using inclusive language** - Avoid language that alienates or demeans

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- **Harassment** - Unwelcome comments or conduct based on protected characteristics
- **Discrimination** - Exclusion or unfair treatment of any individual or group
- **Intimidation** - Threats, aggressive behavior, or deliberate exclusion
- **Abuse** - Personal attacks, insults, or derogatory remarks
- **Unwelcome advances** - Sexual or romantic advances where not appropriate
- **Disruption** - Deliberate disruption of discussions or events

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all spaces managed by the Vitte project, including:

- GitHub repositories and discussions
- Issue trackers and pull requests
- Community forums and chat channels
- In-person events and meetings
- Official social media accounts
- Any representation of the project in public spaces

## Enforcement

Community members who violate this Code of Conduct may face consequences determined by project maintainers, including:

- Warning
- Temporary suspension from participation
- Permanent removal from the community

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:

1. **Contact privately** - Email conduct@vitte-lang.dev with details
2. **Provide context** - Include dates, times, and people involved
3. **Keep it confidential** - Your report will be handled with discretion
4. **Trust us** - We will investigate and respond appropriately

**Do not** confront the person publicly unless you feel safe doing so.

## Handling Reports

When we receive a report:

- We acknowledge receipt within 48 hours
- We investigate the incident thoroughly
- We communicate our findings to the reporter
- We take appropriate action
- We maintain confidentiality of the reporter
